Manage and track support tickets efficiently.

How to Create a Ticket

  1. Click the Create Ticket button above or go to the "Create" page.
  2. Enter a clear and short title for your issue.
  3. Provide a detailed description explaining the problem.
  4. Select the appropriate category for your issue.
  5. Click Submit to create your ticket.

Once submitted, your ticket will appear in the Tickets page where you can track, update, or delete it.
